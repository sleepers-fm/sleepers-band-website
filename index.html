About the Band

Sleepers emerged from an underground basement with a sound that defies categorization. Blending heavy riffs with ethereal bodies, we create music that awakens the soul and stirs the spirit.

Born from evening jam sessions and fueled by continous improvement, our music tells stories of dreams, rebellion, and the search for meaning. Each song is a journey through soundscapes that range from thunderous to sublime.

We're not just a band – we're a collective of artists committed to pushing boundaries and creating experiences that resonate long after the last note fades.
